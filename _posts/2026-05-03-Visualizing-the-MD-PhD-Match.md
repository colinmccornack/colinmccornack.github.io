Every year, the AAMC publishes yearly data on various metrics of The Match, including MD-PhD numbers in ACGME-accredited specialties in their Report on Residents, Table B4. As I approach the end of my PhD, I wanted to explore to see trends in MD/PhD graduates in various specialties, [so I combined the available reports into one combined data table, which can be found here](https://docs.google.com/spreadsheets/d/1J4K8jrtcZGh5sfINoER7WhcENUjd57EAxHsJ1qA1E0c/edit?usp=sharing). I compiled the 2016–2024 data across three report files and built an interactive area diagram to explore how the number of MD/PhD trainees in specialties changes over time.
